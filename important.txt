$mail->Username: Your Gmail address (e.g., your-email@gmail.com).

$mail->Password: Your Gmail app password (if using 2FA) or your Gmail account password.

$mail->setFrom(): Sets the sender's email and the application name (e.g., Your Application Name).

$mail->SMTPSecure: Use PHPMailer::ENCRYPTION_STARTTLS for TLS encryption.

$mail->Port: Use port 587 for TLS.
